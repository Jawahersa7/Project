Generating Knowledge and Awareness

At our company, we talked to people about the problems blind individuals face in public places.

We used a questionnaire to gather their thoughts.

Most said it’s hard for blind people to move around because there aren’t enough tools to help them, and they often feel unsafe.

They also want something that gives them directions in real-time and helps them be independent without needing help all the time.

Many also mentioned that public places should be better designed for blind people. These ideas helped us create a product that solves these problems.
