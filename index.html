Generating Knowledge and Awareness

At our company, we talked to people about the problems blind individuals face in public places.

We used a questionnaire to gather their thoughts. Most said it’s hard for blind people to move around because there aren’t enough tools to help them, and they often feel unsafe.

They also want something that gives them directions in real-time and helps them be independent without needing help all the time.

These insights helped us create a product that solves these problems.

Approach to Innovation

Our product, VisionDirection, is a pair of smart glasses designed to help people who are visually impaired. These glasses use a camera to detect obstacles and provide real-time audio directions through headphones, allowing users to move more confidently and safely.

Product Specifications:

  • Camera: Detects surroundings and relays information
  • Audio Feedback: Provides instructions via headphones
  • AI Technology: Enhances navigation based on environment
